Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026 · Open-source product template; have legal counsel review before commercial operation.

What we collect

  • Account: email and display name when you sign in to cloud features.
  • Workspace data: project files and settings you choose to store in the cloud.
  • Usage metrics: AI request counts for quota (not your API keys or chat content).
  • Local data: API keys and editor preferences stay in the browser unless you enable cloud sync.

What we do not collect

API keys and conversations go directly to your chosen AI provider. We do not proxy them on our servers unless we announce otherwise.

Storage

Cloud data is stored in managed PostgreSQL (e.g. Neon). Local data uses IndexedDB / localStorage.

Your rights

You may request export or deletion of your account and workspaces via the project maintainers (see the repo README).

Contact

Questions via GitHub Issues on the project repository.
